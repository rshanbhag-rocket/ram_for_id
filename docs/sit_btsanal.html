Business Transaction Services Analysis situations

The situations in this category alert you to error conditions related to Business Transaction Services (BTS) activities or processes.

CICSplex_Activity_Warning
BTS activity has terminated or has been forced.

The Business Transaction Services activity identified in the alert is in an ABENDED or FORCED state, indicating that it has not completed normally. Check the application associated with this activity to ensure that the data it processes is valid.

Formula: If the value of CICSplex_BTS_Activity_Details.Completion_status does not equal Normal and the value of CICSplex_BTS_Activity_Details.Completion_status does not equal Incomplete then situation CICSplex_Activity_Warning is true.

CICSplex_ProcessType_Warning
ProcessType is not enabled.

The ProcessType in the alert is not enabled. Business Transaction Services do not allow new processes of this type to be defined until its state is reset to enabled.

Formula: If the value of CICSplex_Web_Service_Details.Status does not equal Inservice then situation CICSplex_ProcessType_Warning is true.
