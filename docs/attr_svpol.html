CICSplex Service Level Analysis SVPOL attribute group

The CICSplex Service Level Analysis SVPOL attributes are used by the enhanced 3270 UI to provide a summary of service policy. These attributes are not available from Tivoli Enterprise Portal.

SPNAME Indicates the eight-character service policy name of the service policy.

STATUS The status of the service policy. Indicates the service policy status: Active, or Inactive.

DESCRIPTIndicates the description of the service policy.
